# Tool Manifesto Template (Hybrid Format)

## 1. Title & Tagline
<Insert tool name> — <Insert striking, identity-level tagline>

---

## 2. Opening Narrative (Purpose & Context)
Describe:
- The context in which this tool exists  
- The problem it solves  
- The shift in thinking the reader must adopt  
- Why now? Why this? Why it matters?  

Tone: narrative, philosophical, accessible.

---

## 3. Identity & Core Essence (DIL-Aligned)
- **Essence (what it *is*)**
- **Functional identity (what it *does*)**
- **Human identity projection (how people will talk about it)**
- **Role in the ecosystem**
- **Contextual footprint (EchoForge / LTF / PW / EnaC relationships)**

---

## 4. Core Principles of the Tool
List 5–10 “truths” or “laws” that the tool embodies.

---

## 5. What This Tool Solves
Explain the cognitive, structural, or workflow problems this tool eliminates.

---

## 6. Its Role in the Ecosystem
Define:
- Its place in the EchoForge → LTF → Phoenix Workbench flow  
- Its interdependencies  
- How it contributes to the AI-human co-creation environment  

---

## 7. Human-Facing Benefits
Describe:
- Increased clarity  
- Reduced cognitive load  
- Improved workflow  
- Narrative empowerment  

---

## 8. AI-Facing Behavioral Directives
Explain how AI should:
- Interpret this tool  
- Maintain alignment  
- Behave when operating through it  
- Use the tool’s constraints and signals  

---

## 9. Interoperation Rules (EnaC / ECL / Tools)
Describe interaction patterns with:
- EnaCs  
- ECL layers  
- Adjacent tools  
- The semantic ecosystem  

---

## 10. Patterns of Use
Guidelines for when to use the tool:
- Triggers  
- Scenarios  
- Anti-patterns  

---

## 11. Boundaries & Non-Purposes
Clarify what the tool **must not** be used for.

---

## 12. Evolution Path (TEO + 5-EAR-Aware)
Describe how the tool’s meaning and structure will evolve safely across:
- Semantic changes  
- Structural refinement  
- Contextual expansion  
- Role diversification  

---

## 13. Closing Vision Statement
A strong final message capturing the long-term purpose of the tool.
