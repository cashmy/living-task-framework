### CIP-E (5 Components - Correct)

* *C - Context**: Situation, background, constraints
- Example: "Want to start business, not sure where to begin"
- AI extracts: First-time entrepreneur, exploratory phase

* *I - Intent**: What user wants to accomplish
- Example: Business startup guidance
- AI extracts: Need structured approach, reduce overwhelm

* *P - Purpose**: What change will result (outcome, effect, impact)
- Example: Launch viable business
- AI extracts: Economic independence, fulfillment

* *E - Emotion**: Emotional state and tone influence (control vector)
- Example: Uncertainty, excitement
- AI extracts: Need confidence-building, encourage exploration

* *Ev - Evolution**: Continuity across sessions (prevents amnesia)
- Example: First exploration â†’ later implementation
- AI maintains: Original vision, constraints discovered, decisions made

* *How It Works**:
- AI performs CIP-E extraction **internally** (not shown to user)
- Extraction guides **which questions to ask**
- User sees **only the questions** (not the reasoning)
- Questions designed to fill gaps in C-I-P-E-Ev understanding
- Answers used to generate hyper-tailored solutions
